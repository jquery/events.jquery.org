Three days of learning about - and contributing to - real open source projects with peers and mentors from the jQuery community.

The jQuery Developer Summit is a three-day event aimed at making open source accessible to, and inclusive of, members of underrepresented groups Participants of all skill levels and disciplines will learn best practices from jQuery open source contributors, and will leave having made contributions to one of the jQuery Foundation's projects.
